Ongoing Research Projects

Project 1: Investigation of multi-states of pentaquark in three different regions using Thomas Fermi Quark Model

On this project, we are trying to develop a mathematical model of multi-states of pentaquarks with the help of Thomas Fermi Quark Model. Here, the quarks are assumed to lie on three different regions. Analytically and numerically, differential equations for both the light and heavy quarks are solved.
